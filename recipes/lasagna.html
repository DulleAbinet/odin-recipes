Lasagna is my Favorit food

My Layers of Lasagna

A mouthwatering delight that’s smell seems to seduce you away from
anything else you have planned for that moment. The layers of red sauce and cheeses,
perfectly dispersed over the fine ground beef that’s cut to perfection
by my mother’s precise hands. The potent smell of garlic seems to fight its way through all the other
ingredients rushing into your nose. Each layer from top to bottom
getting gradually heftier, paralleling the human person; the farther you dig,
the more meat, cheese, and tomatoes you get.
The classic taste of my mother’s lasagna has a far deeper meaning than the dish shows,
due to the fact of taste and smell, appearance, and preparation.

Ingredients to make lasagna

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Bulk Italian sausage
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese
  • Egg, lightly beaten
  • Tomato paste
  • Water
